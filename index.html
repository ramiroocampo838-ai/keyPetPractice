Game Features

Everything you need to raise your perfect desktop companion

Keyboard Detection

Every keystroke earns XP. Type documents, code, or chat — your pet rewards it all.

Mouse Tracking

Clicks and movements count too. Your pet follows your cursor and reacts playfully.

XP & Points System

Level up your companion and unlock new abilities, animations, and rare items.

In-Game Shop

Spend points on outfits, hats, food, toys, and special accessories for your pet.

Pet Evolution

Watch your companion grow and evolve through stages. Each evolution unlocks new forms.

Mood System

Your pet has emotions! Feed it, play with it, and keep it happy for bonus rewards.

Sleep Mode

When you rest, so does your pet. Idle animations and dream sequences await.

Lightweight Widget

Non-intrusive and super light. Works silently in the background with minimal resources.
